Breakfast Potatoes

Description

Breakfast Potato is a simple, hearty skillet dish made with potatoes and bacon—or any preferred protein—cooked to golden perfection. It's filling enough to serve as a full meal, with or without extras like bell peppers or sausages. Best made in a cast iron skillet for that perfect crisp, it can also be finished in the oven for extra crunch. The potatoes remain the true stars of this satisfying breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 5 Big Potatoes (Irish)
  • 200g Bacon, diced
  • 300g Sausage
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper Chopped
  • 1 Green Pepper Chopped
  • 2 Tablespoons Coconut/ Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Small Onion chopped
  • 1 Tablespoon Paprika( Optional)
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Tablespoon Minced Ginger
  • 1 Teaspoon Rosemary
  • 1 Teaspoon Dried Thyme
  • 2 Knorr Chicken cubes
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Prep the Potatoes: Wash, peel, and dice the potatoes into bite-sized cubes. Parboil for 5 - 7 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. Fry the Bacon and Sausage: Heat oil in a skillet. Add diced bacon and sausage, and fry until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté the Aromatics: In the same skillet, add chopped onions, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until fragrant.
  4. Add Potatoes and Seasonings: Add the parboiled potatoes. Sprinkle in rosemary, thyme, paprika, chicken cubes, and salt. Stir and cook until the potatoes begin to brown.
  5. Mix in Peppers and Protein: Add the chopped red and green peppers, along with the fried bacon and sausage. Stir well and cook for another 5 - 10 minutes until everything is golden and well combined.
  6. Finish and Serve: Taste and adjust seasoning. Serve hot as a complete, satisfying breakfast or brunch dish.
